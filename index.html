PROCESS · 流儀

How I work

Three operating principles.

AI as a real collaborator

Not autocomplete. Claude Code and Dify are how I move from spec to working build in one evening. The skill is in the prompt, not the typing.

Async-first, written-first

I work in long-form documents and Upwork chat. No video calls if avoidable. Kyoto timezone (UTC+9), but most async-friendly clients won't notice.

Ship, then refine

MVP in three days, polish in week two. The first version is ugly on purpose — it forces decisions about what actually matters.
